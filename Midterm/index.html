Welcome to 7U

What We Do

The internet has given the world unprecedented access to more educational content than ever before. Anyone can get a great education for just the price of a basic internet connection. However, the sheer quantity of educational content alone makes it hard to navigate. At 7U we gather high quality educational resources from across the internet and organize them into courses from an introductory level all the way to an advanced level. We do all the hard work and searching so that you don't have to.

How it works

Courses are divided into intro, intermediate, and advanced levels. When possible we try to equate these to middle school, high school, and college levels, respectively. "Classes" with 7U consist of a series of educational videos and sometimes supplemental reading content. 7U lets you operate at your own pace and thus learn very fast, although we recommend slowing down and taking the time to fully understand each course.
